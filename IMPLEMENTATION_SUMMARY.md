## 🎯 Marketing Strategy

### The Funnel:
```
1. Pop-up appears on site load (3 seconds)
   ↓ (60% engagement rate target)
2. User clicks to spin wheel
   ↓ (80% of spins complete)
3. Offer reveals with promotional code
   ↓ (50% click claim button)
4. User redirected to contact form
   ↓ (70% form completion)
5. Business follows up with customer
   ↓ (Convert to sale)
```

### Why This Works:
- **Gamification:** Games are 3x more engaging than static content
- **FOMO:** Limited time offer creates urgency
- **Free Value:** Free domain is concrete benefit
- **Low Friction:** Easy to close, not annoying
- **Clear Path:** Obvious next step (contact form)
